^YMandelbrot^0

Program control:

^WUP^0 .......... move up
^WLEFT^0 ........ move left
^WRIGHT^0 ....... move right
^WDOWN^0 ........ move down
^WA+UP^0 ........ zoom in
^WA+DOWN^0 ...... zoom out
^WA+RIGHT^0 ..... increase number of steps
^WA+LEFT^0 ...... decrease number of steps

Blinking LED indicates render completed.
